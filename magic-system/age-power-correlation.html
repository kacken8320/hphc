Age-Power Correlation

The older a wizard, the more powerful they get. Power is dependent on: age, wisdom, practice, knowledge, ...
Different to muggles, wizards grow stronger in every aspect with age. (strength, height, hearing, sight, smell, reaction time, ...)
This doesn't mean they grow younger and physically stronger with time, they stay old, weak and slow, until is comes to spontaneous magical skill expression.

Complete Power

Complete Power is being increased by age (complete_power *= log(age)). This means, the older the wizard, the more powerful all their stats get. As this increase is logarithmic, the older they get, the less powerful is the multiplier (without maths: the older they get, the slower they increase in all skills). A wizard makes more progress from age 10 to 20 than from age 250 to 260.

Height

Wizard's grow their whole life. They roughly follow log7(x), where x is the age. The normal fluctuations between human beings stays (so roughly +-20cm).

  • Age 10 -> ~1.2m
  • Age 20 -> ~1.5m
  • Age 40 -> ~1.9m
  • Age 50 -> ~2.0m
  • Age 75 -> ~2.2m
  • Age 100 -> ~2.4m
  • Age 150 -> ~2.6m
  • Age 200 -> ~2.7m
  • Age 250 -> ~2.8m
  • Age 300 -> ~2.9m

Life Expectancy

Probability a wizard is dead at age x:
(Only considering natural death)

  • Age 50 -> ~0%
  • Age 75 -> ~1%
  • Age 85 -> ~5%
  • Age 100 -> ~10%
  • Age 125 -> ~30%
  • Age 150 -> ~60%
  • Age 175 -> ~80%
  • Age 200 -> ~90%
  • Age 225 -> ~95%
  • Age 250 -> ~99%
  • Age 275 -> ~99.9%
  • Age 300 -> ~99.99%
That means, every 100th wizard makes it to 250 years, every 1000th to 275 years, and every 10000th to 300 years.

Reaction Time

Wizards reaction times decrease with age.

  • Age 10 -> ~2sec
  • Age 20 -> ~1.5sec
  • Age 30 -> ~1sec
  • Age 40 -> ~0.5sec
  • Age 50 -> ~0.1sec
  • Age 60 -> ~0sec
  • Age 70 -> ~0.1sec future
  • Age 80 -> ~0.25sec future
  • Age 90 -> ~0.5sec future
  • Age 100 -> ~1sec future
  • Age 200 -> ~2sec future
  • Age 300 -> ~3sec future
Don't think of these reaction times as a 300 year old grandpa catching a fly with chopsticks. Think of it as a shift in the air before a storm breaks, or an echo of something that's yet to happen. It is a deeply magical awareness, like an intuition or a sense.
